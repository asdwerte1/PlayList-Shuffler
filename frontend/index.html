Shufflify

Shuffle your playlists - bias free

What is this?

A simple app designed to shuffle your Spotify playlists

The Spotify shuffle algorithm often puts bias on songs you play often leading to hearing the same songs over and over

By using this, you will give your playlists a randomised order, creating a more true shuffe experience

How do I use it?

First click the authenticate button below - this will ask you to sign into Spotify and grant permission to access your acount

Your playlists will then be displayed below

Click the playlist you wish to shuffle, a confirmation will appear when this is done

Go to the playlist in Spotify and ensure the sort is set to "Custom", then simply play from the top without shuffle enabled
